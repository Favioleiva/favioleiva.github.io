Research Progress Report · 9 Jul 2026 · GSID, Nagoya University
Moran's Spaghetti: Exploratory Space-Time Data Analysis for High-N, High-T Panels
An ESTDA workflow that tracks global and local Moran's I across space and time. Using an animated "Country of Bees" simulation, the local Moran's I spaghetti plot reveals two localized mining booms hidden inside a large N×T panel.

Research Progress Report · 7 Jul 2026 · GSID, Nagoya University
From Spaghetti Plots to Fan Charts: Descriptive Statistics for High-N, High-T Panels
A visual approach to describing large panel datasets — moving from dense "spaghetti" line plots to descriptive decile fan charts, illustrated with daily Black Marble nighttime lights for Peru, Bolivia, and Indonesia (2012–2026).
